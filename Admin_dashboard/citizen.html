Process Flow

How Our System Works

A smooth and transparent journey designed to turn citizen concerns into real solutions.

1

Create Your Account

Begin by registering using your mobile number. Secure verification ensures authenticity while your personal profile helps us connect your concerns with the right support channels.

2

Submit Your Concern

Clearly explain the issue you are facing, choose the appropriate category, and enhance your report by uploading images or videos along with the exact location.

3

Monitor Progress

Every submission receives a unique reference ID, allowing you to follow updates, view actions taken, and stay informed throughout the resolution process.

4

Receive Resolution

Dedicated teams coordinate with responsible authorities to address the matter efficiently, keeping you notified until the issue is successfully resolved.
